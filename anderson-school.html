The Anderson School

Visit this uniquely repurposed Junior High School in downtown Bothell for food, entertainment, and lodging.

The Anderson School in Bothell, Washington, is a historic landmark transformed into a vibrant community hub.

Here’s a detailed summary:

Background

The Anderson School, originally built in 1931 as a junior high school, has been repurposed by McMenamins into a unique destination offering a variety of attractions. The renovation preserves the building’s historic charm while introducing modern amenities.

Features of the Anderson School

  • Hotel: The former school now houses a hotel with uniquely decorated rooms, each with its own story and connection to local history.
  • Restaurants and Bars: Several dining options are available, including a brewpub, a fine dining restaurant, and several bars, each offering a distinct ambiance and menu.
  • Movie Theater: The property includes a small movie theater that shows a mix of new releases, classic films, and special screenings.
  • Pool: The North Shore Lagoon, a large indoor pool, offers a tropical-themed swimming experience, open to both hotel guests and the public.
  • Event Spaces: Various rooms and areas are available for private events, meetings, and community gatherings.

Activities and Attractions

  • Dining and Socializing: The diverse range of restaurants and bars makes the Anderson School a popular spot for dining out and social gatherings.
  • Movie Nights: The on-site theater provides entertainment with a variety of film showings, including family-friendly options and themed nights.
  • Swimming: The North Shore Lagoon is a unique indoor pool experience, providing fun for families and a relaxing escape for adults.
  • Live Music and Events: The venue hosts live music performances, trivia nights, and other community events, creating a lively atmosphere.

Impact on the Community

  • Historic Preservation: The renovation of the Anderson School preserves a significant piece of Bothell’s history, maintaining its architectural integrity while adapting it for contemporary use.
  • Economic Boost: The Anderson School attracts visitors to Bothell, supporting local businesses and contributing to the area’s economic growth.
  • Community Gathering Place: It serves as a central location for community events and activities, fostering social connections and community spirit.
  • Tourism: As a unique destination, it draws tourists to Bothell, showcasing the town’s charm and increasing its visibility.

Local Significance

The Anderson School stands as a testament to Bothell’s ability to blend historical preservation with modern innovation. It provides a dynamic space for residents and visitors to enjoy a wide range of activities, from dining and entertainment to relaxation and social events. For the community, it represents a cherished landmark and a vibrant addition to Bothell’s cultural landscape.
